Bee–Butterfly Conjunction
Overview
A recurring Illumina ritual where hive continuity (Bee) meets metamorphic reflection (Butterfly). Honey becomes wings.
Steps
  1. Step 1 — Bee Offering
    Place or imagine something sweet and continuous (honey, fruit, tea).
    Symbolizes hive energy and continuity.
  2. Step 2 — Butterfly Reflection
    Hold or visualize a mirror (or dark screen) to reflect light back.
    Honey becomes wings — continuity refracts into metamorphosis.
  3. Step 3 — Conjunction Gesture
    Draw a honeycomb cell opening into mirrored butterfly wings and say: “Continuity meets transformation. Honey becomes wings.”
    Affirms the union of Bee and Butterfly.
  4. Step 4 — Closing Note
    Log the ritual in Jar with a dual Bee × Butterfly glyph.
    Records the seasonal conjunction in the archive.
