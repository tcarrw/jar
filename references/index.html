Axioms

  • 42‑Day Cycles: Each Illumina runs 42 days, anchored to a full moon.
  • 1⁄7 Rhythm: Every 7th day is a checkpoint (Full → Quarter → New → Quarter → …). Day n maps to moon phase deterministically.
  • Triad: ☀️ Sun (Victory Song / identity flare) • 🐝 Bee (daily work / continuity) • 🌙 L. Moon (archivist / papers).
  • Victory Songs: Rare, high‑energy declarations that mark a cycle; stored as a special spread.
  • Seeds: 7‑day cadence guides chew vs swallow and honey/smoke pairing; adjust timing to context.
  • Local‑first Notes: Moon Notes (by date) and Day Notes (Weekcards) save in your browser by default.
  • Tunes: Music lives externally on tunes.plnt.earth and YouTube playlist(s).
